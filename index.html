About Our Company

Established in the year 1998, We Swethas Interior Decors, are one reputed suppliers and dealers of Interior Products and doing lot of turn key projects in and around of Tamil Nadu, Andhra Pradesh and Pondicherry. Our comprehensive range comprises All types of Flooring (i.e. Carpet, Vinyl flooring, Wooden flooring, Tiles etc…), False Ceiling (i.e. Gypsum board, POP, 2’x 2’ Ceiling, Thermacole, Fibre glass wool etc…), Window Coverings (i.e. Vertical Blinds, Wooden Venetian Blinds, Roller Blinds, Wooden Blinds etc…) All types of Paintings, Office Chairs, Furniture, All types of Glass, SS Hand Rail, ACP work, Audio & Visual Solutions and Carpentry works etc…
